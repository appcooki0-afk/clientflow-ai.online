Lead Management

How to Receive Leads

Your form submissions are managed through Formspree. When someone fills out the contact form on your website, you will receive their information via email and in your Formspree dashboard.

Current Form URL: https://formspree.io/f/xpwzgdpj

Go to Formspree →

Setup Formspree (One-time)

  1. Go to formspree.io and create a free account
  2. Click "New Form" to create a form
  3. Name it "ClientFlow Leads" or similar
  4. Copy your form endpoint (it looks like: https://formspree.io/f/xxxxxx)
  5. Open index.html and replace xpwzgdpj with your form ID
  6. Upload the updated files to GitHub
Note: The form currently sends data to a placeholder form ID. You need to create your own Formspree form to receive submissions.

What Happens When Someone Submits?

✓ You get an email notification with all their details

✓ The lead appears in your Formspree dashboard

✓ You can export leads to CSV/Excel

✓ Set up automatic email forwards to your phone
